LooksIQ Account Deletion Request

Developer: Sohel Sheikh

This page explains how to request deletion of your LooksIQ account and associated data.

Option 1: Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open LooksIQ.
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Type DELETE to confirm.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us at help.looksiq@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: Delete my LooksIQ account
  • The email address used to sign in
  • (Optional) Your full name and last date you used the app
Processing time: In-app deletions are processed immediately. Email requests are processed within 30 days after verification.

Data deleted

  • Authentication account and profile
  • Meals, meal items, and nutrition entries
  • Skin scans and stored scan photos
  • AI summaries and coach reports
  • Subscription status records

Data retained

  • Local copies stored on your device remain until you uninstall the app or clear its storage.
  • We do not retain server-stored account data after deletion is completed.
